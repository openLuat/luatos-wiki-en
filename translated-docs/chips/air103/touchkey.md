# Touch sensor/touch button

This document corresponds to the `touchkey` library. Only air101/air103 support this function.

|  2      | PA_09|
|  3      | PA_10|
|  4      | PB_00|	
|  5      | PB_01|		
|  6      | PB_02|		
|  7      | PB_03|		
|  8      | PB_04|		
|  9      | PB_05|		
|  10      | PB_06|		
|  11      | PB_07|	
|  12      | PB_08|

|  14      | PA_12|
|  15      | PA_14|
